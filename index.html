Set in a time where the Neolithic Revolution was bringing people together to form the first Civilizations, lead the history of a family throughout the ages from the Ancient times to the Classic Era building villages, forging alliances, fighting enemies and writing your own history.

An RTS inspired to the 90s classics of historical games packed with realism and set in a large living world.

Continental Map

Continuous real-time persistent map. A living world with seasons, day-cycle and an inter-connected ecosystem.

Deep Character Profile

Each in-game person has a unique personality, needs, skills, thoughts, health conditions, relationships and their own back story.

Realistic AI Decision Making

People and animals make decisions based on deep behavior workflows prioritising their needs on their individual goals and coordinating each other through social groups and organizations.

Real Economy

Every object, every plant, every animal, every person in the game is only a product of the game itself. The value of each object depends solely on its demand and supply, no automatic spawning, inflation caps or any artificial modification of in-game balance.

Historical Cultures

Each in-game person belongs to a culture, this affects their beliefs and ways of life. Historical cultures selected from real life Late Neolithic include known ones such as Romans and Gauls and less known ones like Umbrians and Peloponnese.

Social Policies

Each social group is regulated by a number of policies which define the dos and don’ts of that particular community going from how property is managed (shared or private), the rights and duties of women and men (matriarchal or patriarchal policies), attitude towards foreigners, punishments for breaching the law.
